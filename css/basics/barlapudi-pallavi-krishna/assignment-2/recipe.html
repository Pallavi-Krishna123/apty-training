Dragonfire Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g enchanted rice noodles
  • 1 tbsp Drogonfire chili oil
  • 2 cloves garlic(minced)
  • 1 small Phoenix pepper(finely sliced)
  • 100g stir-fried shadow mushrooms
  • 1 tsp soy sauce brewed under a blood moon
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Directions

  1. Boil the enchanted noodles in salted water until soft, then drain.
  2. In a hot pan, stir-fry garlic and Phoenix pepper in Dragonfire oil.
  3. Add the mushrooms and soy sauce, cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Toss in the noodles and mix until evenly coated.
  5. Serve hot with fresh basil and a sprinkle of flame crystals (optional).

A legendary spicy noodle dish infused with mystical heat!

Warning:Only true spice warriors may handle the heat!
