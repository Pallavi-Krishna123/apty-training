Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated parmesan (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and cooked pasta to the pan.
  4. Toss well and garnish with parsley and parmesan.
  5. Serve warm.

No-Bake Oreo Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 20 Oreo cookies
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 250g cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup whipped cream
  • More Oreos for topping

Preparation Steps

  1. Crush Oreos and mix with melted butter. Press into a pan as base.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Fold in whipped cream and crushed Oreos.
  4. Spread mixture on the base and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  5. Top with crushed Oreos and serve chilled.

Chicken Ramen Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1 chicken breast, grilled & sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Green onions & sesame seeds

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pot, heat chicken broth, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Add cooked noodles to serving bowls and pour broth on top.
  4. Top with sliced chicken, boiled egg halves, green onions, and sesame seeds.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
