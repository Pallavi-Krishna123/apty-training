Dragonfire Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g enchanted rice noodles
  • 1 tbsp Dragonfire chili oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 Phoenix pepper (sliced)
  • 100g stir-fried shadow mushrooms
  • 1 tsp moon-brewed soy sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Dragonfire Noodles Image

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until soft, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat chili oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and Phoenix pepper.
  3. Add mushrooms and soy sauce; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add noodles and toss until well coated with the mixture.
  5. Garnish with basil leaves and serve hot!
